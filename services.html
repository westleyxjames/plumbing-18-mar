Our Services

Comprehensive plumbing solutions for residential and commercial properties

At PrimeLine Plumbing, we offer comprehensive plumbing services for both residential and commercial properties throughout Manhattan and surrounding NYC areas. Our experienced technicians are equipped to handle everything from routine maintenance to complex installations and emergency repairs.

Complete Plumbing Solutions

🔧

Plumbing Repairs

Our technicians handle a wide range of plumbing repairs including faucet fixes, pipe repairs, and fixture replacements. We diagnose issues thoroughly and provide clear explanations of the work needed.

Common repairs include fixing dripping faucets, replacing worn washers, repairing toilet mechanisms, fixing garbage disposals, and addressing various pipe issues. We also handle more complex repairs like slab leaks and corroded pipes.

Estimated Cost: $150 - $500

*Pricing varies based on the specific repair needed and parts required

💧

Drain Cleaning

Professional drain cleaning services to address clogs and slow drains in sinks, tubs, and main lines. We use appropriate equipment and techniques based on the severity and location of the blockage.

Services include clearing kitchen and bathroom sink drains, bathtub and shower drains, main sewer line cleaning, and preventive maintenance to reduce future clogs. We use video inspection when needed to identify underlying issues.

Estimated Cost: $125 - $400

*Pricing depends on the location and severity of the clog

🔥

Water Heater Installation & Repair

Installation, maintenance, and repair services for water heaters. We work with various types including tankless and traditional models, ensuring proper installation and safe operation.

Services include new water heater installation, repairs to existing units, routine maintenance, thermostat adjustments, and troubleshooting issues like insufficient hot water or strange noises. We can help you choose the right size and type for your needs.

Estimated Cost: $500 - $2,500

*Installation costs vary by unit type and complexity. Repairs typically range $200-$600

🔍

Leak Detection

Thorough leak detection services to identify hidden water leaks that could cause damage. We use specialized tools and techniques to locate leaks in walls, under floors, and in underground pipes.

Our leak detection services help prevent water damage, reduce water bills, and identify issues before they become major problems. We can detect leaks in supply lines, drain lines, and fixtures using advanced technology.

Estimated Cost: $200 - $600

*Cost varies based on detection complexity. Repair costs are separate

⚠️

Emergency Plumbing Services

Available for urgent plumbing situations such as burst pipes, major leaks, or sewage backups. Our team responds to emergency calls to help minimize disruption to your home or business.

Emergency services are available 24/7 for situations that require immediate attention to prevent property damage or health hazards. We prioritize urgent calls and work quickly to resolve critical issues and restore normal function.

Estimated Cost: $200 - $800+

*Emergency service rates apply. Final cost depends on the issue and time of service

🏢

Commercial Plumbing Solutions

Plumbing services tailored for commercial properties including offices, restaurants, and retail spaces. We understand the importance of minimizing disruption to your business operations.

Commercial services include routine maintenance, repairs, installation of commercial fixtures, grease trap maintenance, backflow prevention, emergency response for businesses, and compliance with commercial plumbing codes.

Custom Quote Required

*Commercial pricing varies by project scope. Contact us for a detailed quote

🔨

Pipe Repair & Replacement

Services for repairing or replacing damaged pipes including copper, PVC, and galvanized pipes. We address issues like corrosion, cracks, and outdated piping systems.

Whether you need a section of pipe repaired or a complete repiping project, we provide solutions that restore proper function and prevent future problems. We also offer pipe relining for certain situations.

Estimated Cost: $300 - $1,500+

*Cost depends on pipe material, length, and accessibility

💧

Fixture Installation

Professional installation of plumbing fixtures including faucets, sinks, toilets, showers, and bathtubs. We ensure proper installation for optimal performance and longevity.

Whether you're updating your bathroom or kitchen, or installing new fixtures in a renovation project, our team handles installation with precision. We can also recommend fixtures that fit your needs and budget.

Estimated Cost: $200 - $800

*Cost varies by fixture type and installation complexity. Fixture cost separate
